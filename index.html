Biography

I received my PhD degree at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Wai Lam. I earned my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Shandong University, supervised by Prof. Zhumin Chen. In the past, I also interned at Ant Group, Tencent AI Lab, and CPII.

My research interests focus on domain adaptation and out-of-domain generalization for LLMs, advanced decoding strategies, and the development of multimodal agents for real-world deployment.
